About Me

The person
behind the work

I start my mornings early — journal first, then meditation, then the day. This rhythm keeps me sane through the highs and lows of PhD life. I believe deeply in intentional living: knowing why you do what you do, and doing it fully.

I love golf for its patience, the piano for its peace, and the gym for its honesty. Books are my constant companions — especially those that challenge how I see myself and the world. I'm drawn to ideas about growth, resilience, and what it means to live a meaningful life.

I believe in giving back quietly. Helping people not because anyone is watching, but because it's the right thing to do. Some of the most important things we do are the ones no one sees.
