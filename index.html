Research

Draft 2026

Price Discrimination and Gender Inequities in Health Insurance: Evidence from the ACA's Gender Rating Ban

Studies the impact of the ACA's ban on gender-based pricing in health insurance using ACS data (2010–2018). Implements difference-in-differences and triple-differences designs exploiting cross-state variation in pre-policy gender rating practices. Finds that the reform increased nongroup insurance coverage among women in states with higher pre-policy exposure.

Submitted to APPAM Annual Conference, 2026

Draft 2026

Generative AI and Firm Hiring Demand: Evidence from Global Job Postings

Examines how the release of ChatGPT affected firm hiring demand across countries using global job posting data. Uses a continuous difference-in-differences design with predetermined firm-country exposure based on 2019 occupational composition. Finds heterogeneous dynamics across advanced and developing economies.

Submitted to JEBO Special Issue on Generative AI and Economics, 2026

Work in progress

Geographic Mobility and Smoking Behavior: Evidence from the PSID

Uses panel data from the PSID to estimate how relocation across regions with different smoking environments affects individual smoking behavior.
